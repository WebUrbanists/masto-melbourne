masto.melbourne

Welcome to masto.melbourne, the newest Mastodon instance designed specifically for users in the beautiful city of Melbourne, Australia! We are excited to provide a safe and inclusive platform for Melbournians to connect, share, and communicate with each other.

About Us

At masto.melbourne, we value community, respect, and diversity. Our goal is to provide a welcoming and supportive space for people of all backgrounds, identities, and beliefs. We encourage open discussion and constructive dialogue while maintaining a harassment-free environment.

Features

local_library

Local Timeline

The local timeline displays posts from users in Melbourne, allowing you to connect with others in your area and keep up with local events and news.

add_reaction

Custom Emoji

We offer a unique set of custom emoji specific to Melbourne that you can use in your posts and conversations.

add_moderator

Active Moderation

Our team of moderators is dedicated to maintaining a safe and inclusive environment for all users. We have a clear set of community guidelines and a reporting system to address any issues that may arise.

mindfulness

Local Focus

masto.melbourne is designed with a local focus, so you can connect with other users who share your interests and experiences in Melbourne. From local events to community happenings, our platform provides a space to stay up-to-date and engaged with your city. Whether you're a local or new to the area, masto.melbourne offers a unique opportunity to connect with like-minded people in your community.
