About Us

Welcome to masto.melbourne! We are a Mastodon instance designed specifically for users in Melbourne, Australia. Our platform is built around community, respect, and diversity, and we are committed to providing a safe and inclusive space for all users.

At masto.melbourne, we believe that social media should be a tool for connection and communication, not a source of negativity or division. Our team is dedicated to fostering a positive and supportive environment where users can engage in constructive dialogue, build relationships, and share their interests and experiences.

Our platform features a local timeline that displays posts from users in Melbourne, allowing you to connect with others in your area and stay up-to-date on local events and news. We also offer a unique set of custom emoji specific to Melbourne that you can use in your posts and conversations.

Our team of moderators is committed to maintaining a safe and inclusive environment for all users. We have a clear set of community guidelines and a reporting system to address any issues that may arise. We take your privacy seriously and have strict policies in place to protect your personal information.

At masto.melbourne, we are excited to provide a space where users can come together to connect, share, and communicate. We invite you to join our community and help us build a vibrant and supportive space for all Melbournians.

Code of Conduct

masto.melbourne is committed to providing a positive and inclusive space for our users. We do not tolerate discrimination, harassment, or any kind of drama on our platform.

We welcome a variety of discussions and encourage respectful and constructive dialogue among our community. We reserve the right to remove any content that violates our content policy, including but not limited to:

  • Discrimination or harassment based on race, gender, sexual orientation, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic
  • Threats of violence or harm
  • Spam or irrelevant content
  • Illegal or harmful activities
  • Pornography or sexually explicit content
  • We also reserve the right to suspend or terminate the accounts of users who violate our content policy.

We are dedicated to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all of our users, and we appreciate your support in upholding our content policy

While we wish we could, we cannot add every possible rule, and because of this we must mention that if we deem your behaviour or your actions not fit for this server, we have the right to punish.

Support Us

As a community-owned and operated platform, we rely on the support of our users to continue providing a positive and inclusive space for discussions and connections. We are currently accepting financial contributions from our users to help us maintain and improve our platform.

Your contributions will go towards things like hosting costs, technical support, and community engagement initiatives.

By financially supporting masto.melbourne, you are not only helping to keep our platform running, but you are also supporting a community-owned alternative to large, commercial social media platforms. Your contributions will help us continue to provide a positive and engaging space for urbanists and city enthusiasts to connect and share ideas.

If you are able, we encourage you to consider making a financial contribution to masto.melbourne. Every little bit helps, and we are grateful for your support. You can find more information about how to contribute on our website. Thank you for being a part of the masto.melbourne community, and for your support in keeping our platform running.
